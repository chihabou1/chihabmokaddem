About Me

Hello!

I received a Bachelor in Computer Science and a Master in Software and Information Systems Engineering from the Université de Mostaganem, Algeria. During this period, I was part of the SIF (Simulation, Integration & Data Mining) Research Team & the LIO (Lab of Informatics of Oran) Lab of the Université d'Oran, Algeria. I completed my Ph.D. at the Université de Montréal, Canada, under the direction of Prof. Houari Sahraoui and Dr. Eugene Syriani. At this time, I was a member of the GEODES (Software Engineering Research Group) team.
My research interests include Search-based software engineering, Refactoring, Design pattern detection, Model transformation, and Model-driven engineering. My academic background includes teaching experience at the Université de Montréal and at the National institute of professional training of Algeria. I have also been involved in reviewing tasks in different journals and conferences.
